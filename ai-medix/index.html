WHAT YOU GET WITH AI MEDIX

Your personalized heart health plan

Backed by the latest research, designed just for you. No stress required.

Effective medications

We prescribe medications we know will work for you. Most are generic, though we provide insurance navigation for brand name medications. We also review and adjust existing prescriptions.

Ongoing data monitoring

Your plan includes a blood pressure monitor and/or scale that automatically shares your data with your AI Medix care team. They check your readings daily and alert you if anything is wrong.

Simple daily action plans

To keep you moving forward, the AI Medix app gives you simple actions to complete every day — covering everything from exercise and medication to taking your blood pressure.

Expert diet and lifestyle support

Looking for more than medication? Your AI Medix care team includes a registered dietitian to help you with food choice, calorie tracking, guidance on physical activity, and more.

Meet the AI Medix team

Your dedicated AI Medix care team includes a doctor, a physician assistant, and a dietitian, all giving you support and attention at every step of the way.

OUR CLINICAL APPROACH

The secret to better heart health? It isn’t a secret at all.

We know that better heart health is the key to living longer, and that preventing and managing certain conditions is the path to a healthier heart. Yet millions of Americans aren’t getting the right care. They’re taking the wrong medications, waiting months for doctor’s appointments, and navigating lifestyle changes without support. That’s where we come in.

At AI Medix, we take a proactive and comprehensive approach to the conditions at the center of heart health — because that’s how you get the outcome you deserve.
