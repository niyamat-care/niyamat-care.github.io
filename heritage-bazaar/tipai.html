Where the wild roam free

Turn off the alarm.
Set aside the itinerary. Breakfast will be served whenever you are ready. The only thing you must do is what you want, when you want to.

Unfettered by the rules of the typical wildlife holiday, Tipai crafts a truly bespoke experience in the untouched forests of Tipeshwar.

The perfect escape

At Tipai, you needn’t leave the lap of nature to enjoy that of luxury

Designed in tune with the natural landscape, Tipai offers guests a choice of villas in a pocket of wilderness all to themselves. Built from the very land they stand on, our rammed earth and stone villas celebrate local materials and craftsmanship.

Pool Residency

FROM 39,000 INR
NIGHTLY

Soak in a world of your own

Unhindered views, expansive spaces and absolute privacy — each residence spans 2172 sq ft and includes a sprawling master suite with both indoor and outdoor living spaces. Located along the buffer zone of the Tipeshwar Wildlife Sanctuary and the rivulet that meanders between us and the forest, wildlife sightings aren’t limited to safaris for our pool residents!

Sundowners by the plunge pool, sunshine in your private gazebo and the luxury of time that glides at your pace. With a personal butler at your service, every detail of your holiday is perfected with care.

A holiday for every reason, a forest for every season

No two stays at Tipai are ever the same. For those in pursuit of more than the thrill of a sighting, we invite you to enjoy the wild your way.

Do as much or as little as you want. And if you have something specific in mind, allow us to craft a bespoke time for you.

Reimagine the wildlife getaway with us

Host

Celebrate significant moments and the bonds you share with Tipai. We design your stay to make your milestones memorable, from intimate anniversaries to joyous reunions and fabulous weddings to special birthdays.
Get together at Tipai.

Savour

Go beyond the menu at Tipai. From private chef’s tables to cooking classes, and wine tastings to celebrity supper clubs, we create culinary experiences for the gourmand in you. Tell us what you have in mind and we’ll do our best to make it happen.

Getaway

Wildlife like never before. Journey into the wilderness with our naturalist on your private safari, equipped with powerful binoculars and cameras for each of you. Enjoy a sundowner at our machaans overlooking haunts popular with Tipeshwar’s fauna. Dwell in the joy of the jungle.

Retreat

Revel in the luxury of your own space in the wild. Relax with a range of therapies at the spa or at your villa. For those who would like a deeper retreat, talk to us about our curated wellness experiences. Private/group yoga or other wellness immersions can be organised on request.

Learn

Follow your favourite photographer with your telephoto lens in tow. Take field notes with our in-house naturalist. Discover folk art in our neighbourhood. Invite your guru for a painting retreat. Learn the intricacies of the perfect cocktail with a mixologist.

Find a new passion or explore an enduring one. Tipai is an ideal setting for people brought together by a shared desire to learn.

170 km from the closest city of Nagpur, Tipeshwar is a true getaway. A thriving biodiversity hotspot, it is home to a plethora flora with more than 182 species of birds and animals, including the Bengal Tiger.

Four rivers crisscross the 148.63 sqkm sanctuary in Yavatmal district of Maharashtra —Bhima, Purna, Krishna and Tapti. Waterfalls and reservoirs punctuate the densely forested basalt lands.
