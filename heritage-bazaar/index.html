Welcome to Heritage Bazaar

Journey with us in pursuit of the rare

Where time stops, when you want to. Where every experience pays rare attention to you.

Soak in the richness of India's handmade looms with every Pochampalli saree, where tradition and skill intertwine to produce a masterpiece. Each saree reflects the labor of love that goes into mastering the ikat weaving technique—an ancient skill that requires immense precision and patience. The threads are meticulously tied, dyed, and then woven by hand, resulting in patterns that are symmetrical, bold, and mesmerizing. The use of pure silk or cotton adds to the luxurious feel, making every Pochampalli saree a statement of elegance and heritage.
